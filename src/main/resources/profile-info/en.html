🎬 FFmpeg Profile Overview for Videoconverter

🎥 Normal

Standard quality for general purposes. Good balance between quality and file size.

-c:v libx264 -preset slow -crf 23 -c:a aac -b:a 192k

🎮 Replay Mod Recording

Optimized for Minecraft Replay Mod recordings. High frame rate and quality.

-c:v libx264 -preset fast -crf 18 -pix_fmt yuv420p -r 60 -c:a aac -b:a 192k

📈 High Quality (x264)

Very high quality with slow processing. Ideal for high-quality archiving.

-c:v libx264 -preset veryslow -crf 18 -c:a aac -b:a 256k

⚡ Fast Encoding (x264)

Very fast encoding with lower quality. For quick previews or tests.

-c:v libx264 -preset ultrafast -crf 28 -c:a aac -b:a 128k

🖥️ NVIDIA GPU (h264_nvenc)

Hardware-accelerated H.264 encoding with NVIDIA NVENC. Fast and efficient.

-c:v h264_nvenc -preset p4 -cq 23 -b:v 5M -c:a aac -b:a 192k

📱 YouTube / Mobile Optimized

Optimized for uploads to YouTube and mobile devices. Compatible format with "faststart".

-c:v libx264 -preset slow -crf 23 -pix_fmt yuv420p -movflags +faststart -c:a aac -b:a 160k

💾 Small File (Low Quality)

Very small file size at the expense of image quality. Useful for space-saving applications.

-c:v libx264 -preset veryfast -crf 32 -c:a aac -b:a 96k

🧼 Lossless (x264)

Lossless encoding with x264. Perfect for mastering or archiving without quality loss.

-c:v libx264 -preset veryslow -crf 0 -c:a flac

🌈 HEVC (x265)

H.265 / HEVC software encoding with good compression and quality. Requires compatible devices.

-c:v libx265 -preset slow -crf 28 -c:a aac -b:a 160k

🔥 NVIDIA GPU (HEVC)

Hardware-accelerated HEVC encoding with NVIDIA NVENC. For fast, efficient encoding.

-c:v hevc_nvenc -preset p5 -cq 28 -c:a aac -b:a 160k

🎮 Twitch Recording

Optimized profile for livestream recordings at 30 FPS. Good quality at low bitrate.

-c:v libx264 -preset faster -crf 22 -r 30 -c:a aac -b:a 128k

📸 No Audio

Extracts video only without audio track. Useful for silent clips or video editing.

-an -c:v libx264 -preset medium -crf 23

🎧 Audio Only (MP3)

Extracts audio only and encodes it as MP3. Perfect for music, podcasts, etc.

-vn -c:a libmp3lame -b:a 192k

🧪 Benchmark (No Output)

Test profile that produces no file output. Useful for performance measurement (encoding only, no writing).

-f null -

📼 DVD Interlaced (MPEG-2)

Generates an interlaced MPEG-2 video in PAL-DVD format. Suitable for older DVD players and TVs.

-target pal-dvd -c:v mpeg2video -c:a ac3 -b:a 192k

🐢 Very High Quality (Slow Upload)

Extremely high quality with very slow encoding. Ideal for archiving or high-quality uploads.

-c:v libx264 -preset veryslow -crf 16 -c:a aac -b:a 320k

🆕 AV1 (Standard Quality)

Good visual quality with modern compression. Recommended for web videos with small file size.

-c:v libaom-av1 -crf 30 -b:v 0 -preset medium -c:a libopus -b:a 128k

🆕 AV1 (High Quality)

High quality AV1 encoding with lower CRF value. Slow processing but better quality.

-c:v libaom-av1 -crf 23 -b:v 0 -preset slow -c:a libopus -b:a 192k

🆕 AV1 (NVIDIA NVENC)

Fast AV1 encoding with NVIDIA RTX 30xx+ GPUs. Good performance with good quality.

-c:v av1_nvenc -cq 28 -preset p5 -c:a aac -b:a 160k

🌐 AV1 for YouTube/Web

Optimized for streaming platforms like YouTube. Good quality, modern AV1 compression, streamable with +faststart.

-c:v libaom-av1 -crf 30 -b:v 0 -preset good -pix_fmt yuv420p -movflags +faststart -c:a libopus -b:a 160k

💾 Archival (Lossless FFV1)

Lossless archival with the FFV1 codec (recommended for long-term archiving). Audio in FLAC.

-c:v ffv1 -level 3 -c:a flac

📱 Social Media (Mobile)

For vertical video (9:16) on platforms like Instagram, TikTok, etc. 720x1280 resolution at 30 FPS.

-c:v libx264 -preset fast -crf 24 -vf scale=720:1280 -r 30 -pix_fmt yuv420p -c:a aac -b:a 128k

⚙️ Proxy (Superfast Preview)

Extremely fast encoding for preview files or quick transcodes with low quality.

-c:v libx264 -preset ultrafast -crf 35 -c:a aac -b:a 96k

🔄 Remux (No Re-encoding)

Changes only the container format without re-encoding. Fast, lossless process.

-c copy

🎧 Audio Only (Opus)

Extracts audio only and encodes it space-efficiently in modern Opus format.

-vn -c:a libopus -b:a 128k

🌐 VP9 (WebM)

Uses VP9 codec for WebM output. Good compression, but AV1 is generally more efficient.

-c:v libvpx-vp9 -crf 32 -b:v 0 -c:a libopus -b:a 128k

⏱️ Split by 10 Min

Automatically splits video into new segments every 10 minutes. Ideal for longer content or chapter separation.

-f segment -segment_time 600 -reset_timestamps 1 -c:v libx264 -crf 23 -preset medium -c:a aac -b:a 128k

🖥️ Screen Capture (Lossless)

High-quality, lossless screen capture with RGB color space. Perfect for tutorials or software demos.

-c:v libx264rgb -preset ultrafast -crf 0 -pix_fmt rgb24 -c:a flac

🖼️ GIF (Palette Optimized)

Creates an optimized color palette for GIF export. Must be run before the final GIF pass.

-vf fps=10,scale=480:-1:flags=lanczos,palettegen -y palette.png

🖼️ GIF (Final)

Uses the previously generated palette to create the final GIF with reduced colors.

-i palette.png -lavfi "fps=10,scale=480:-1:flags=lanczos [x]; [x][1:v] paletteuse" -loop 0

📺 MPEG-2 (Set-Top Box)

Encoding for old set-top boxes or MPEG-2 compatible devices. Low compression, high compatibility.

-c:v mpeg2video -q:v 5 -c:a mp2 -b:a 192k -target pal-dvd

🔍 Visually Lossless (x264)

Nearly lossless encoding with CRF 16. For quality-conscious users who still want reasonable file sizes.

-c:v libx264 -preset slow -crf 16 -c:a aac -b:a 256k

🎮 Gaming (NVIDIA AV1, High FPS)

High frame rate gaming videos (e.g., 120 FPS) with NVENC-AV1. Good quality with smooth playback.

-c:v av1_nvenc -preset p4 -cq 23 -rc vbr -b:v 8M -r 120 -c:a aac -b:a 160k

🎙️ Podcast / YouTube Audio Normalized

Normalizes audio loudness for podcasts and YouTube, ensuring consistent volume levels. Video is copied unchanged.

-af loudnorm=I=-16:TP=-1.5:LRA=11 -c:v copy -c:a aac -b:a 192k

🎥 Two-Pass (1080p @ 4Mbit) - Pass 1

First pass of two-pass encoding for 1080p video at 4 Mbps bitrate. No audio output, output discarded (analysis only).

-c:v libx264 -b:v 4M -pass 1 -an -f mp4 /dev/null

🎥 Two-Pass (1080p @ 4Mbit) - Pass 2 (Final)

Second pass of two-pass encoding for precise bitrate and better quality at 4 Mbps. Includes AAC audio at 192 kbps.

-c:v libx264 -b:v 4M -pass 2 -c:a aac -b:a 192k

⬜ Square Format (1:1)

Scales video to square format 720x720, ideal for Instagram or other social media, with medium compression quality.

-vf scale=720:720 -c:v libx264 -preset medium -crf 23 -pix_fmt yuv420p -c:a aac -b:a 128k
